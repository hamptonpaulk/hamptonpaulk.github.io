The Imaginary Council

Written by Hampton Paulk on Thu 19 June 2014

Napoleon Hill said that as a part of his rituals of pursuit he did the following.

"Every night, over a long period of years, I held an imaginary Council meeting with this group whom I called my -Invisible Counselors."

These group of folks are mine.

Kurt Vonnegut, Dewey Ray Prescott, Richard Feynman, Henry Rollins, Ralph Waldo Emerson, & Walt Whitman.
